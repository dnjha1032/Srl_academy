Our Exclusive Teaching Methodology

The present trend of various competitive exams, primarily focuses on the applicability of one’s understanding of the concepts in diverse situations and creativity, and with very few questions testing the student’s memorizing capacity.

Our promoters also felt that, along with the mandatory clarity of concepts, swiftness and speed is also vital in overcoming the strict time constraints imposed in answering the paper and excelling in the exam

Our methodology, therefore (keeping these viewpoints) lays emphasis on the following aspects.

Delivery of lecture is 100% interactive, thus ensuring student’s engagement as well as involvement.

Topics will be discussed in a way the student understands the concepts thoroughly.

The student is encouraged to discusses/clarify any doubts without any hesitation.

Numerous examples based on real life situations will be discussed in the class to help the student improve his application skills and clarity of concepts.

After ensuring that the student is crystal clear with the concepts and is independently able to solve the problems, necessary tips & shortcuts (wherever relevant) will be provided through a learning path.

This is to encourage them to answer questions and solve problems independently and to think creatively and complete the questions in time

Homework assignments will be given after completion of each topic. Wide spectrum of problems from various sources will be given to make the Student more sure and confident.

Periodic parent Teacher meetings/counseling sessions will be conducted to ensure that the student’s performance is kept under check and to enhance his/ her performance
